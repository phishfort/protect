The Protect add-on does not use any analytics or spy on your activity in any way. It does not require you to create a user account or sign into it to use it. You can use the add-on completely anonymously. PhishFort will never share your email address with anyone for any reason.

The only time that information is collected from users is when reporting a malicious URL. Only the information necessary to perform the takedown of the malicious content is collected, namely the suspicious URL and the victim URL, and any additional comment submitted by the user.
